About Me

I'm a product designer working on various projects for lots of clients. My skillset lies on creating branding packages & websites to deliver the full user experience for new and also veteran businesses.

You can often find me creating videos about design work on YouTube, or sharing my thoughts on my podcast, Dialogue With Designers. I'm passionate about giving back and teaching what I know to the next generation of designers.
